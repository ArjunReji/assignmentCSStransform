2D Transforms

translate()

This moves the element 50 pixels to the right and 100 pixels down.

rotate()

This rotates the element 45 degrees clockwise.

scale()

This scales the element to twice its original size in both width and height.

skewX()

This skews the element 20 degrees along the X-axis

skewY()

This skews the element 20 degrees along the Y-axis

3D Transforms

translate3d()

This moves the element 50 pixels to the right, 100 pixels down, and 50 pixels forward on the Z-axis.

rotate3d()

This spins the image 45 degrees around a line that passes through its center, tilted 45 degrees between the x and y axes.

rotateX()

This rotates the element 45 degrees around the X-axis

rotateY()

This rotates the element 45 degrees around the Y-axis.

rotateZ()

This rotates the element 45 degrees around the Z-axis

scale3d()

This scales the element to twice its original size along the X-axis.

Combination Effects

rotateX() and rotateY()

Rotates the image around the X-axis (horizontally) by 360 degrees and rotates the image around the Y-axis (vertically) by 180 degrees.

scale3d() and skewX()

image will become wider along the X-axis and slightly shorter along the Y-axis and it will be tilted diagonally along the X-axis.

translate3d() and rotate3d()

image smoothly transitions from its original state to a state where it is scaled differently along the X and Y axes, skewed along the X-axis, and then moves and rotates in 3D space on hover.
